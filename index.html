Free Pictures you can use anywhere

Pixapic has a thriving creative community that exchanges free images. All of Pixapic's content has been made available under the Content Licence, making it safe to use for a variety of commercial endeavours without seeking authorization or providing credit to the creator.

What to Know More?
What are Free Images?

Royalty-free images have a one-time fee, predetermined restrictions, are unlimited in terms of usage, and are stored in an image gallery. In contrast, copyright-free images have no fee, no restrictions, are unlimited in terms of usage, and are stored in the public domain.

What is allowed?

Use of every image on Pixapic is free.
There is no need to attribute. Although not required, giving credit to the photographer or Pixapic is always appreciated.
The Pixapic images can be edited. Edit them in any way you see fit.

What is not allowed?

Identifiable individuals may not be portrayed negatively or in an offensive manner.
Never offer unaltered copies of a photograph for sale as a poster, print, or on a tangible item.
Don't imply on the imagery that people or brands are endorsing your product.
Don't share or sell the images on other stock image or wallpaper marketplaces.
